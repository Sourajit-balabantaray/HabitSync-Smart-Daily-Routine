Morning Workout Plan

Follow these steps:

  • Wake up consistently – Get 7–8 hours of sleep
  • Hydrate & warm up – Drink water, light stretching
  • Set your goal – Strength, cardio, or flexibility
  • Workout session – 20–45 min focused exercise
  • Short breaks – Rest between sets
  • Cool down – Stretch and relax muscles
