Evening Workout Plan

Prefer moderate intensity:

  • Warm up gently – 5–8 min mobility
  • Main set – 20–35 min (avoid max intensity)
  • Rest between sets – Keep breathing controlled
  • Hydrate – Small sips only
  • Cool down – Stretch + slow breathing
  • Post-workout – Shower and start winding down
