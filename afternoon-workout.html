Afternoon Workout Plan

Keep energy steady:

  • Light snack (optional) – If you’re low on energy
  • Warm up – 5–8 min mobility + easy movement
  • Main workout – 25–45 min (strength/cardio)
  • Rest properly – 45–90 sec between sets
  • Hydrate – Small sips during training
  • Cool down – Stretch + slow breathing
