Evening Relax Plan

Slow down and prepare for sleep:

  • Dim lights – Make the room calm
  • Limit screens – Reduce phone/social media
  • Light activity – Music, reading, or a short walk
  • Breathe & unwind – 5 minutes slow breathing
  • Reflect briefly – Clear your mind and plan tomorrow
